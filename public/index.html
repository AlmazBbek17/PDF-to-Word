faq

Questions people actually ask

What file comes out of this?

A standard .docx file that opens in Word, Google Docs, or LibreOffice — nothing proprietary.

Does it work on scanned documents?

Yes. Scanned and photographed pages go through an OCR pipeline before conversion, including tilted or lower-quality scans.

Is my file stored anywhere?

No. Files are processed in memory and deleted immediately after conversion — see the Privacy Policy for specifics.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes, anytime, directly from the paywall screen in the extension. You keep access until the end of the billing period.

What languages does it support?

The interface is available in 10 languages. Documents themselves can be in virtually any language — the model preserves the original wording rather than translating it.
