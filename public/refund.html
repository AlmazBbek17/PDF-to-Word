Refund Policy

Last updated: July 23, 2026
This is a starting template, not legal advice. Refund and cancellation rules are subject to consumer-protection law in the buyer's country (the EU's 14-day withdrawal right for digital goods is a common example) — confirm your policy is compliant with a lawyer, and check Dodo Payments' own merchant-of-record requirements before publishing.

1. Try Before You Pay

Every user gets 10 free pages with no sign-in and no payment required, specifically so you can evaluate accuracy on your own documents before subscribing.

2. Subscription Refunds

If you're unhappy with your subscription within 7 days of your first payment, contact us at lettergen1@gmail.com and we'll issue a full refund, no questions asked. After that window, subscription payments are generally non-refundable, except as required by applicable law.

3. Cancelling Your Subscription

You can cancel your subscription at any time from within the extension. Cancelling stops future renewals — you keep access to your plan's page limit until the end of the period you already paid for. We don't offer prorated refunds for partial months.

4. Failed or Broken Conversions

If a conversion fails due to a technical error on our end and the pages are deducted from your limit, contact lettergen1@gmail.com with the approximate time of the failed attempt — we'll restore the pages to your balance.

5. How Refunds Are Issued

Approved refunds are processed by Dodo Payments back to your original payment method and typically appear within 5–10 business days, depending on your bank or card issuer.

6. Chargebacks

If you believe you were charged in error, please contact us directly first — most billing issues are resolved faster this way than through a chargeback with your bank.

7. Contact

Billing questions: lettergen1@gmail.com
