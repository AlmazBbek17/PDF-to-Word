This Privacy Policy explains what information PDF to Word ("we," "us") collects through the PDF to Word browser extension and related services (the "Service"), and how it's used.

1. What We Collect

  • Files you upload. PDF documents you choose to convert are sent to our servers for processing and are deleted immediately after conversion completes. We do not retain, back up, or use uploaded files for any purpose beyond producing your converted document.
  • Account information. If you sign in to subscribe to a paid plan, we receive your email address via Google Sign-In. We do not receive your Google password or access your Google account beyond basic profile information.
  • Usage data. We track how many pages you've converted against your plan's limit, so we can enforce free-tier and subscription limits. Free-tier users are identified by a random, non-personal identifier stored in your browser — not by any personally identifying information.
  • Billing information. Payments are processed by Dodo Payments, our payment processor and merchant of record. We do not receive or store your full card details — see Section 4.
  • Technical data. Standard server logs (timestamps, error messages, rate-limit data) are kept briefly for debugging and abuse prevention.

2. What We Don't Collect

  • We do not sell your data to third parties.
  • We do not use your documents to train any AI model.
  • We do not run advertising trackers or third-party analytics cookies.

3. How Your File Is Processed

When you convert a PDF, page images are sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis, and — for scanned pages — processed locally on our server via an OCR engine (Tesseract) before being sent for analysis. The resulting Word document is generated on our server and streamed back to your browser. No copy of your file or its content is retained once the conversion request completes.

4. Third-Party Services We Use

  • Anthropic (Claude API) — processes page images to extract text and structure. Governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Google (Sign-In) — used only to verify your identity and obtain your email address for account creation.
  • Dodo Payments — our payment processor and merchant of record, handling all subscription billing. Governed by Dodo Payments' Privacy Policy.
  • Railway — our hosting provider for the backend infrastructure.

5. Data Retention

Uploaded files: deleted immediately after processing. Account data (email, subscription status, page-usage counters): retained while your account is active, and deleted upon account deletion request. Server logs: retained for a limited period for debugging and security purposes, then automatically purged.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at lettergen1@gmail.com.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

The extension uses browser local storage (not third-party cookies) to remember your session, language preference, and a random anonymous identifier for free-tier usage tracking. None of this is used for advertising or cross-site tracking.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
